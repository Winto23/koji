Koji Cafe & Bao

Welcome to Koji Café, your cozy corner of comfort in the heart of the city. Inspired by the Japanese concept of "kōji," meaning "nurturing," we offer a warm, inviting sanctuary where the hustle and bustle of everyday life melts away. Our sustainably sourced, fair-trade coffee is roasted to perfection, and our menu features delicious options from freshly baked pastries to hearty salads, all made with locally sourced ingredients. Enjoy our comfortable seating, soft lighting, and thoughtful decor, perfect for relaxation and conversation. Join our vibrant community with events like open mic nights and coffee workshops.

About Us

Welcome to Koji Cafe and Bao, your cozy retreat in the heart of South Okala,Yangon. Founded by a team of food enthusiasts passionate about authentic flavors and innovative culinary techniques, we blend the rich traditions of Asian cuisine with a modern, inviting atmosphere. Our menu features a variety of handcrafted baos made with the finest ingredients, paired perfectly with our expertly brewed coffee sourced from the best beans around the world. Designed as a relaxing escape from the hustle and bustle, Koji offers a warm, welcoming ambiance ideal for catching up with friends or enjoying a quiet moment. Committed to sustainability and supporting our local community, we prioritize eco-friendly practices and local sourcing. Thank you for choosing Koji Cafe and Bao; we look forward to serving you and making your visit memorable. Welcome to the Koji family!
